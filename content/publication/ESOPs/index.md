abstract: We study employee stock ownership programs (ESOPs), which 10.9 million US workers have access to, providing them equity in their employer. In event studies in US administrative data (the LEHD), we are examining the impacts of the introduction of these programs on wages and retention. We also plan to look at how they affect retirement timing and firm productivity.
